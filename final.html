The game is called DONT LOSE ! This is a short three level game that might require a tremendous amount of skill and patience. Many might be familiar to this game as it originates from cool math "the worlds hardest game". The goal of the game is to get the square to the other blue square in order to get to the next level. The player must use his/her Up, Down, Right, Left arrows to move the square to he desired place. Howver be careful there are moving obstacles all over the levels and each level brings its own twist!!!
